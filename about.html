Hi, I'm Elizabeth!

I'm a passionate and technically skilled Visual Designer with experience creating internal and external communication collateral and providing design direction that fits with - and enhances - organizational brands. I pride myself in being an exceptional communicator, able to work with cross-functional teams and build loyal relationships with clients while collaborating on projects. I am fluent in the Adobe Creative Suite and have a growing knowledge in Marketing and User Experience.

The concept of Web Design depends on the future goals of Website owners. It's got to be created with an end user in mind. I ensure that systems, interfaces, language and graphics are user-friendly, emotive, aesthaetically pleasing, clear on-brand and usable with a touch of mellow smoothness. As a Web Designer/Developer with budding information architecture and interactive design skills, I mostly work with HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, Reactjs and Ruby.
